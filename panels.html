Select Your Lab Panel

Diabetes Prevention Panel

This comprehensive blood panel evaluates key markers associated with blood sugar regulation and metabolic health. It helps identify early risk factors for diabetes and prediabetes, allowing individuals to take preventive steps before symptoms develop.

Testing may include markers related to glucose metabolism, long-term blood sugar control, and overall metabolic function. Early detection can support lifestyle changes and proactive health management.

Recommended for:
Adults with a family history of diabetes, individuals experiencing fatigue or weight changes, or anyone interested in monitoring metabolic health.

$149

This panel includes tests for hemoglobin A1C, fasting glucose, insulin, and comprehensive metabolic panel to detect early blood sugar abnormalities.

Cancer Risk Panel

This wellness screening panel evaluates several blood markers that may indicate abnormalities in organ function, inflammation, and overall cellular health. While not a diagnostic test for cancer, these markers can sometimes reveal early changes that warrant further medical evaluation.

Routine blood screening plays an important role in early detection and preventive health care.

Recommended for:
Adults interested in proactive health monitoring or individuals with a family history of cancer.

$199

Monitors inflammation and abnormal markers with CBC, comprehensive metabolic panel, CRP, and LDH tests.

Kidney Health Panel

The Kidney Function Panel evaluates key biomarkers that help assess how well your kidneys are filtering waste and maintaining proper fluid and electrolyte balance. This screening can help identify early signs of kidney stress or reduced kidney function.

Regular testing can support early detection and help individuals monitor kidney health as part of a proactive wellness plan.

Recommended for:
Individuals with diabetes, high blood pressure, a family history of kidney disease, or anyone interested in monitoring kidney health

$129

Checks kidney function with creatinine, BUN, eGFR, and electrolyte tests.

Heart Health Panel

This panel evaluates important markers associated with heart and vascular health. It provides insight into cholesterol levels, lipid balance, and other indicators that may contribute to cardiovascular risk.

Monitoring these markers can help identify early warning signs and support heart-healthy lifestyle decisions.

Recommended for:
Adults with a family history of heart disease, high blood pressure, or individuals interested in maintaining cardiovascular health.

$159

Assesses cardiovascular risk with lipid panel, hs-CRP, glucose, and metabolic panel tests.

Comprehensive Wellness Panel

The Comprehensive Wellness Panel provides a broad overview of your overall health by evaluating multiple key biomarkers related to metabolic health, organ function, and general wellness. This panel is designed to help identify potential health concerns early, even before symptoms appear.

Testing may include markers associated with blood sugar regulation, kidney and liver function, electrolyte balance, and other indicators that reflect how well your body’s major systems are functioning. Regular screening can provide valuable insight into your current health status and help support proactive, preventive healthcare decisions.

This panel is ideal for individuals who want a detailed snapshot of their health, monitor existing health concerns, or establish a baseline for long-term wellness tracking.

Recommended for:
Adults seeking a comprehensive evaluation of their overall health and individuals interested in preventive health monitoring.

$249

Full body screening including CBC, lipid panel, metabolic panel, hemoglobin A1C, thyroid, and vitamin D tests.
