Understanding Your Lab Panels

Diabetes Prevention Panel

This panel includes tests for hemoglobin A1C, fasting glucose, insulin, and comprehensive metabolic panel to detect early blood sugar abnormalities.

Cancer Risk Panel

Monitors inflammation and abnormal markers with CBC, comprehensive metabolic panel, CRP, and LDH tests.

Kidney Health Panel

Checks kidney function with creatinine, BUN, eGFR, and electrolyte tests.

Heart Health Panel

Assesses cardiovascular risk with lipid panel, hs-CRP, glucose, and metabolic panel tests.

Comprehensive Wellness Panel

Full body screening including CBC, lipid panel, metabolic panel, hemoglobin A1C, thyroid, and vitamin D tests.
