Billing & Insurance FAQ

Mims Express LLC – Mobile Phlebotomy Services

Do you bill Medicaid or insurance?

No. Mims Express LLC does not bill Medicaid, Medicare, or private insurance.

Who bills Medicaid or insurance?

The laboratory performing the testing bills Medicaid or insurance directly.

What does Mims Express LLC bill for?

We bill for professional specimen collection services only.

Who receives the invoice?

Invoices are sent to the facility or laboratory, depending on the service agreement.

Why don't you bill insurance directly?

This model ensures compliance, avoids billing delays, and reduces administrative burden for facilities.

Does this affect patient care?

No. This structure allows us to focus on timely, high-quality specimen collection.

Is this common practice?

Yes. This is the industry-standard billing model for mobile phlebotomy providers.
