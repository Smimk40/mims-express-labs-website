Who We Are

Mims Express Labs LLC is a mobile phlebotomy company dedicated to providing safe, convenient, and accurate specimen collection services. We serve nursing homes, clinics, physicians, and private patients with professionalism and care. With 23 years of experience, we have established ourselves as a trusted leader in mobile phlebotomy services.

Our Mission

Our mission is to deliver dependable mobile phlebotomy services that improve access to laboratory testing while maintaining the highest standards of patient care, safety, and compliance.
