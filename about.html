About Mims Express Labs

Bringing professional, compassionate mobile lab testing to your home or facility. Our mission is to make preventive health care accessible, convenient, and reliable for everyone in our community.

Our Story

After experiencing diabetes and stage 3 breast cancer, our founder realized how important early testing can be. Many diseases develop silently before symptoms appear. Mims Express Labs was created to help people detect health problems earlier through preventive lab testing.

Why Choose Us?

  • Licensed, experienced phlebotomists
  • Mobile service—testing at your home, office, or facility
  • Fast, secure results
  • Compassionate, patient-centered care
  • HIPAA-compliant and privacy-focused
