Provider & Physician Lab Orders

Submit a Lab Order

Physicians, clinics, telehealth providers, and healthcare facilities may submit lab orders for patients using the form below. Once submitted, our mobile phlebotomy team will contact the patient to schedule specimen collection.

Important Information

Please upload a signed physician lab requisition when submitting orders. Our mobile phlebotomy service will contact the patient directly to schedule specimen collection and coordinate laboratory processing.
